FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic codinf challanges to help developers improve their frontend skills with projects in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Frontend Mentor offers a variety of free projects to help developers improve their frontend skills with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. However, access to advanced challenges requires a subscription.

Can i use Frontend Mentor Projects in my portfolio?

Yes, you can use Frontend Mentor projects in your portfolio. These projects are designed to help you demonstrate your skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, making them an excellent addition to showcase your development work.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenges?

If you're stuck on a challenge, you can get help by viewing solutions shared by others on GitHub. Additionally, you can seek advice from the community forums or discussion threads.
